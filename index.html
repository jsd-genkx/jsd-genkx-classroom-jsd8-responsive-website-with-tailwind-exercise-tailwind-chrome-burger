Signature Coffee

An iced Americano is a refreshing coffee drink made by diluting espresso with cold water and serving it over ice. The drink has a deep, rich brown color with a slightly frothy surface from the freshly brewed espresso. The espresso gives it a bold, slightly bitter flavor, but the addition of cold water softens its intensity. It's typically served in a clear glass or plastic cup, allowing the dark coffee and ice cubes to be visible, and often enjoyed with a straw or stirred to mix the layers.
